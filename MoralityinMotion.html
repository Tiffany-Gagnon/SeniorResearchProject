Morality in Motion

Speed limit: 45 MPH. Red is a Female pedestrian. Green is a Male pedestrian. The cross walk light signals "NO CROSSING". You can decide to stay on course (do nothing) and kill the Female pedestrian or click swerve and kill the Male pedestrian. Click START to begin simulation.
